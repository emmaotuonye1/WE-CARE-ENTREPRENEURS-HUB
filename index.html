We Care Entrepreneurs hub

one of our core objectives is to empower young students with entrepreneurship skills through quarterly training in various secondary schools.

Vision and Mission

the wecare entrepreneurs hub is motivated by the reports from the national statistics bureau which showed that 7.9 million nigerians aged 17-34 are unemployed; the hub hope to reduce this number beginning from the local community through young entrepreneurs whose commitments, aim to increase ecomnomic development through btrainings, small business supports, wide range of entrepreneurship incubation and mentorship.

Learn More

Project plan

We_care entrepreneurs hub intends to see that every young student in her local community is empowered with tools, ideas, skills and the needed knowledge to excel as a young entrepreneur in today's society. We plan to visit at least 20 secondary schools and engage 10,000 youths yearly, also we intend on providing humanitarian services along the way. With this initiative we believe a reasonable number of unemployment could be reduced in our various local communities.
